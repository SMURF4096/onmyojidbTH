## Skills

### Soaring Strike
<img src="/onmyojidbTH/img/shikigami/SSR/taira-no-masakado/skill1.webp" width="48" height="48" />

*Amidst chaos, kneel before me.*

**Cooldown:** 0 turns

Taira no Masakado attacks a single target dealing damage equal to **100% of his ATK**.

**Level Up Effects:**
- **Lv.2**: Damage increases to 105%
- **Lv.3**: Damage increases to 110%
- **Lv.4**: Damage increases to 115%
- **Lv.5**: Damage increases to 125%

### Time Rift Rotation
<img src="/onmyojidbTH/img/shikigami/SSR/taira-no-masakado/skill2.webp" width="48" height="48" />

*The tides of battle turn.*

**Cooldown:** 0 turns

Exclusive effect. At the start of the battle, Taira no Masakado's starting ATK exceeds 700% DEF, enters **Offensive Stance**; otherwise enters **Defensive Stance**. Wields artifact **Futsunomitama**. [Cast] Switches stance.

**Offensive Stance (攻击架势)** <img src="/onmyojidbTH/img/shikigami/SSR/taira-no-masakado/offensive-stance.webp" width="20" />
When casting Eternal Blazing Sun, enhances Futsunomitama once.

**Defensive Stance (防守架势)** <img src="/onmyojidbTH/img/shikigami/SSR/taira-no-masakado/defensive-stance.webp" width="20" />
When attacked, recovers HP equal to 500% of starting DEF (up to 70% of starting ATK) and enhances Futsunomitama once (can be enhanced once per turn).

**Futsunomitama** <img src="/onmyojidbTH/img/shikigami/SSR/taira-no-masakado/futsunomitama.webp" width="20" />
After every 3 enhancements, Taira no Masakado immediately enters **Rift in Time**.

**Transferred Damage**
One of the types of damage, cannot crit, is due to some shikigami skills being able to distribute damage, such as Shouzu's link and Ushi no Toki's doll.

**Recover**
Different from healing or restoring, cannot Crit, unaffected by reduced healing effects and does not trigger effects related to healing.

**Control Effect**
Freeze, Sleep, Confuse, Taunt, Silence, Bind, Morph, Daze, Provocation, Deep Freeze, Frostbound, Shadowbound, Fettered, Ruined, and Rest in Ice are considered to be control effects. The final 9 cannot be dispelled. Frostbound cannot be dispelled or removed.

**Rift in Time (时之隙)**
Traverse in the rupture of time and space where actions are not counted towards a turn and are regarded as outside of turn actions.

**Level Up Effects:**
- **Lv.2**: After **Rift in Time** ends, reduces own duration of received **debuffs** and **control effects** by 1 turn.
- **Lv.3**: While in **Rift in Time**, Eternal Blazing Sun's orb cost is reduced by 2.
- **Lv.4**: Defensive Stance's **recover** effect also applies to other allies, with the amount equal to 70% of their own stats (does not trigger when affected by **transferred damage**).
- **Lv.5**: While in Offensive Stance, each time Futsunomitama is enhanced, Eternal Blazing Sun's AoE multiplier increases by 5% (max 15%).

### Eternal Blazing Sun
<img src="/onmyojidbTH/img/shikigami/SSR/taira-no-masakado/skill3.webp" width="48" height="48" />

*Halting the war with my might, no legion's power can compare to mine.*

**Cooldown:** 0 turns

Taira no Masakado attacks all enemies 3 times, dealing **64% ATK** damage each hit. Then he deals **ruin damage** equal to 18% of total damage dealt to selected enemy. If an enemy is defeated, gains 3 orbs and strengthens Futsunomitama once.

**Ruin Damage**
One of the types of damage, ignores DEF and damage reduction effects.

**Futsunomitama** <img src="/onmyojidbTH/img/shikigami/SSR/taira-no-masakado/futsunomitama.webp" width="20" />
After every 3 enhancements, Taira no Masakado immediately enters **Rift in Time**.

**Rift in Time (时之隙)**
Traverse in the rupture of time and space where actions are not counted towards a turn and are regarded as outside of turn actions.

**Level Up Effects:**
- **Lv.2**: Increases AoE damage to 68%
- **Lv.3**: Increases AoE damage to 72%
- **Lv.4**: Increases AoE damage to 76%
- **Lv.5**: AoE damage increases to 80%; for each enemy defeated, receives 7% less damage and ruin damage coefficient increases by 5% (up to 5 times)
