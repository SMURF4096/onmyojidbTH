# Asura

A god of battle born from the darkness of the abyss. He seeks to destroy the heavens with his overwhelming power.

## Skills

### Lotus Eyes
Attacks one target dealing **100% damage**.

**Level Up Effects:**
- Lv.2: Damage increased to 105%
- Lv.3: Damage increased to 110%
- Lv.4: Damage increased to 115% 
- Lv.5: Damage increased to 125%

### Demon Eyes
Has a chance to launch a second attack when attacking. Can be triggered at most once per turn.

**Level Up Effects:**
- Lv.2: Trigger chance increased
- Lv.3: Trigger chance increased
- Lv.4: Trigger chance increased
- Lv.5: Can trigger multiple times per turn

### Asura Slash
Attacks all enemies 3 times, each hit dealing **54% damage**. Damage increases with the number of debuffs on enemies.

**Level Up Effects:**
- Lv.2: Damage increased to 58%
- Lv.3: Damage increased to 62%
- Lv.4: Damage increased to 66%
- Lv.5: Damage increased to 70%
